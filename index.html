AEN at a Glance

The Astronomy Enthusiasts in Nepal (AEN) is a community of youths initiated non-profit organization that serves a purpose of fostering a inovative and collaborative approach to spreading astronomy knowledge across Nepal guided by the vision "Lets's Explore Togehter".

AEN aims to connect people of any age for flourishing their love of astronomy and with time aiding in the growth of the astronomical community in Nepal.
